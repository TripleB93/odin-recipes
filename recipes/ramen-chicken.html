Ramen Chicken Recipe

Description

This is a delicious recipe that I cook when I need a fast lunch. All you need is instant ramen, chicken thighs, and some seasoning. You can have the whole thing finished in 20 minutes, and less than 10 if you pre-cook the chicken thighs the night before. Egg and dried seaweed can be added as a bonus to elevate the dish, but it is not necessary.

Ingrediants

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Top Ramen: Ramen Noodles
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. Season chicken thighs with garlic and onion powder
  2. Heat pan over medium heat and coat with olive oil/butter
  3. Cook for 5 minutes on each side. Take off cut into strips
  4. Cook ramen per instructions on the package
  5. Add the cooked chicken to the ramen
  6. Add hard boiled egg, sesame seeds, and seaweed if desired
