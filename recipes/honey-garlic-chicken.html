Slow Cooker Honey Garlic Chicken

Description

This is a quick and easy one pot recipe for Honey Garlic Chicken that only takes 15 minutes to prep and set. The cook time is 3.5 hours and then it is ready to eat. You can add 2 tablespoons of cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons of water at the end to thicken the sauce. The recipe is perfect for the entire family and makes for amazing left over meals.

Ingrediants

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Ketchup
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steps: Sauce

Combine the following ingrediants into a bowl and mix well:

  1. 1/3 Cup Soy Sauce
  2. 1/3 Cup Honey
  3. 1/3 Cup Ketchup
  4. 6 Cloves Garlic
  5. 3 Tablespoons Ginger
  6. 1/2 Teaspoon Sesame Oil

After creating the sauce, simply add everything to slow cooker and cook on low heat for 3.5 hours.
