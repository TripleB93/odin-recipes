Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a perfect dish for fast cooking with leftovers. It is very simple to make and most people will have all the ingrediants lying around in their fridge. It is also a great way to use leftovers white rice from a previous meal. You can add a lot of customization to this meal, but we will go ahead and give you the basic recipe here.

Ingrediants

  • Eggs
  • White Rice
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy Sauce

Directions

  1. Heat pan over medium high heat, coat with olive oil
  2. Add Rice and create a hole in the center
  3. Add ginger and garlic, cook for 2 minutes
  4. Add scrambled eggs to center and mix with rice when 2/3rd cooked
  5. Add soy sauce and mix together
